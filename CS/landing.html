Case Studies..

There is my repository of case studies. Sometimes, when I'm not out having crazy adventures or playing games, I will come acroos something on the internet that bothers me from a UX/UI/Design standpoint. I like to make note of them and try and think of how I would improve said issue. Usually it's just something I do in Photoshop and let go. Sometimes, if its something for a current job, I'll do more.

Case #1:

Target Optical's Eye Exam System
Issue: When I'm looking for eye exams I'm not always the most loyal person. We have a few near us and I'm more interested in booking an appointment for when I have time. I was frustrated that they system wouldn't let me see available times for all the local doctors. I had to navagate to each location to see the times.
Solution: Create a system that would allow me to see all the available times for my doctors within a set range.
