welcome to the ROOK

We are the future of the board gaming community. We are the first board gaming parlor in Cincinnati and want to offer you a truely unique experence for your next game night. Enjoy your favorite snacks made fresh in our kitchen, or enjoy one of the many beers we have on tap, all while you discover your new favorite tabletop games.

We offer the finest beers from our favorite local breweries: Madtree, Rhinegeist, & Listerman. We also have a wide selection of wines from Bent Tree Winery & Vinoklet Winery. If those are not to your liking our barkeeps will happily make you a mixed drink to compliment your evening.
